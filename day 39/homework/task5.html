HOW MANY PROGRAM LANGUAGES ARE RIGHT NOW?

The Historical Encyclopedia of Programming Languages: lists close to 9,000 languages. Other sources: indicate there are over 1,300 programming languages in use worldwide, with some estimates reaching over 8,000. It is important to note that while thousands of programming languages exist, only a smaller subset are widely used in commercial development and have significant communities and resources available. Examples of popular and widely used languages include Python, Java, C++ JavaScript, Go, and Rust.
