Let's talk about cats

Cats are domesticated, carnivorous mammals from the family Felidae, known for being agile predators with sharp claws and excellent night vision. They have lived with humans for thousands of years as companions or pest controllers and can be aloof or affectionate pets. Domestic cats have diverse appearances and personalities, sleep for a large portion of the day to conserve energy, and communicate through a variety of vocalizations and body language like purring, meowing, hissing, and tail movements.

horses

A horse is a large, herbivorous, hoofed mammal with a long mane and tail, known for its strength and speed. Humans have domesticated horses for over 6,000 years, using them for transport, work, and recreation. Horses are social herd animals that belong to the family Equidae, which also includes donkeys and zebras.
